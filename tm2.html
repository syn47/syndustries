sYn47 likes this map as it is a balanced map with good exits, spacing, distances, items, creeps. The Islands should be removed, sYn47 believes, and it's asymmetrical, and some spawns are really, really bad(the Goblin Merchant spawns). 2v2 maps are harder to balance, though, and this one is well done all things considered. Thumbs up.
