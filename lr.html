A great map, sYn47 sees not much wrong with this one. Perhaps a little in favor of Night Elves who have access to level 3, Mercenaries and an expansion without a lot of potential counterplay, it manages to deliver very fun games with very little cheese. sYn47 gives it a Thumbs Up.
